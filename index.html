Our Philosophy

At Wisdom Surgery Clinic, we combine:

  • Clinical precision, with attention to detail at every stage
  • Evidence-based decision-making
  • Transparent communication
  • Respect for every member of the care team
  • A calm, supportive environment

We work closely with referring practitioners and allied health professionals to ensure your treatment pathway is seamless and carefully coordinated. Our commitment is not simply to perform surgery, but to partner with you through the entire journey, with wisdom and care.

What We Value in Our Patients

We believe that surgical outcomes are strongest when care is collaborative. We value patients who:

  • Engage actively in their treatment planning discussions
  • Ask questions and place trust in their chosen healthcare professionals
  • Respect preparation and recovery recommendations
  • Communicate openly and promptly

We believe mutual respect and shared responsibility create the best outcomes.

No Surprises

Your journey, step by step.

At Wisdom Surgery Clinic, your care follows a considered and transparent process. Here's what you can expect.

1

Consultation & Understanding

We begin by listening. Your concerns, medical history, expectations and goals are explored in detail. Clinical examination and appropriate imaging guide the discussion.

2

Collaborative Planning

Treatment options are explained clearly, including risks, benefits, alternatives and likely recovery. You are supported to make an informed decision without pressure.

3

Preparation

Surgical planning is meticulous. Pre-operative instructions are provided clearly and tailored to your procedure and health profile.

4

Surgery

Procedures are performed with precision and discipline, in carefully selected facilities aligned with safety and quality standards.

5

Recovery & Follow-Up

Post-operative care is structured and proactive. We monitor healing, address concerns early, and remain accessible — including a direct number to reach your surgeon.

6

Long-Term Outcome

Where appropriate, we coordinate ongoing care with your referring practitioner and allied health providers after the conclusion of surgery and recovery.

It's an honor to be a partner in your journey.

Common Questions

We love hearing from you — ask away.

Wisdom Surgery Clinic offers a comprehensive range of specialist treatments in Oral & Maxillofacial surgery — including wisdom tooth removal, extraction of difficult teeth near sinuses and nerves, pre-orthodontic surgery, dental implant surgery with sinus lift and bone grafts, orthognathic jaw surgery, biopsies and oral lesion management, and TMJ assessment.

Consultations are available in person or via telehealth.

Face-to-face consultations are available at Greenslopes, Everton Park, Caboolture, and Redcliffe.

Surgery is performed at Greenslopes Private Hospital, North West Private Hospital, Caboolture Private Hospital, and Peninsula Private Hospital.

We require a referral from your GP, Specialist, or Dentist. Once you have that, contact us by phone, email, or through our online form to schedule a consultation. Our team will advise you on consultation fees and any applicable Medicare rebates beforehand.

Dr Anna Raymond is a dual-qualified oral and maxillofacial surgeon with specialist expertise and a genuine commitment to individualised care. Every patient receives clear communication to support informed decisions, a collaborative approach with their other practitioners, and structured follow-up throughout their recovery.

We provide a detailed written quote including item numbers, a clear explanation of surgical fees, hospital and anaesthetic fee information, and your expected out-of-pocket costs — all before any procedure goes ahead. No surprises.

Our team is happy to assist with private health fund enquiries and DVA/Medicare guidance where applicable.

We encourage patients to raise financial questions openly. Informed consent includes financial consent.

We're committed to reducing waste, moving toward paperless systems, and partnering with ethically aligned suppliers. We believe a healthy workplace culture and strong community trust go hand in hand with delivering excellent patient care.
